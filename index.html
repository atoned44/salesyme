SALES TECHNIQUES

1) At the door - IPOQ

  • I - Intro: "Hi there, my name is <>, and I'm here to chat on behalf of BT/EE."
  • P - Purpose: "We've been doing some upgrades in the local area..."
  • O - Offer: "...and I'm here to share with you the exclusive deals we've got on at the moment for broadband, TV and mobiles."
  • Q - Question: "Who are you currently with for your broadband?"

2) Customer Categories:

  1. Friendly (Friendly & Open)
  2. Uncertain (Unsure or Indecisive)
  3. Distracted (Distracted or Busy)
  4. Dominant (Dominant & Assertive)
  5. Skeptical (Skeptical or Cautious)
  6. Anxious (Anxious or Nervous)
  7. Closed (Closed Off)

AKA - FUDD-SAC

Mnemonic (Memory aid/technique):

Mnemonic

"Friendly and Open, Skeptical too,
Busy and Distracted, Indecisive, it's true.
